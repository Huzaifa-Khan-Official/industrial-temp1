Textile

Kam International is one of the top-leading textile manufacturer and exporters from Pakistan. We are capable of producing 100% cotton fabric, organic cotton yarns, woven fabrics, and 100% pure cotton fabrics with a commitment to provide the best quality products, respectively to our clients.

Denim

In 1999, Mekotex established its name in Denim and in a very short period of time become one of the biggest producers, sellers, and exporters of Denim fabrications. We never compromise on our quality and standards; we are one of the largest denim fabric exporters and suppliers in Pakistan.

Our Capabilities

Over the decades of experience, and with the magic of art-technology, KAM International produces the finest design fabrics. We have the capability of manufacturing numerous kinds of fabrics for individual and home use such as Bedding, design fabrics, denim, kitchen, window, bath and much more. As a vertically integrated textile mill, our fabric production capabilities are embellished with state of the art-technology to maintain consistent quality of our products. We specialize in high-quality, bespoke and small-batch cloth evocative of the golden age of textiles. We produce the finest cotton-fiber and provide complete home solutions to the end consumers.
